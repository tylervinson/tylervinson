The catalog above is the proof of concept. Case in point: PCP Provider Search Tool

This answered a short-notice, high-impact need for our Medicare Advantage segment. As our value-based provider base contracted, affected members were at risk of being auto-assigned a new primary care provider, so they needed a fast, accurate way to search the remaining value-based providers and choose one themselves rather than have that choice made for them. Work of that scope would normally take the better part of a week and the better part of a development team collaborating across roles. Instead, one person stood it up in essentially three hours, less than half a day. Needless to say, the impact on that member segment was tremendous.

The constraint now is access. Consumer-tier accounts weren't built for the way we're using them: sustained, work-related, and increasingly touching real business data. An Premium subscription gives the small group doing this work the headroom, security controls, and data protections appropriate for an organization in our industry, rather than stretching personal-grade tools past what they were meant for.

The ask is straightforward: provide an Premium seat for the team member already delivering this work. The track record above, and the PCP turnaround in particular, is the evidence that the spend pays for itself, and that giving the team the right tier lets it produce more of the same, faster and on firmer footing.
