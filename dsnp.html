Compliance Considerations

CMS Regulatory Requirements

Critical Compliance Areas:

All D-SNP marketing materials and website content must comply with CMS Marketing Guidelines for Medicare Advantage plans. Non-compliance can result in penalties or plan termination.

Required Disclaimers & Legal Text

  • CMS approval statement on all marketing pages
  • Non-discrimination clause prominently displayed
  • Grievance and appeals process information
  • Provider network disclaimer and accuracy statements
  • Benefits and coverage limitation disclosures
  • Multi-language accessibility compliance (especially Spanish)

HIPAA and Privacy Compliance

Technical Privacy Requirements:

  • SSL encryption for all form submissions
  • GDPR-compliant cookie consent management
  • Privacy policy specific to healthcare data handling
  • Secure data transmission protocols
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing

Accessibility Requirements

WCAG 2.1 AA Compliance:

Healthcare websites must be accessible to users with disabilities. This includes proper heading structure, alt text, keyboard navigation, and screen reader compatibility.

Accessibility Implementation Checklist

  • Semantic HTML structure with proper heading hierarchy
  • Alternative text for all images and graphics
  • Keyboard navigation functionality for all interactive elements
  • Color contrast ratios meeting WCAG standards (4.5:1 minimum)
  • Screen reader testing and optimization
  • Form labels and error message accessibility
  • Video captions and audio transcriptions
